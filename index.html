Who We Are

The Risk Band is a Charlotte-based band playing classic rock live music in the southeast. Formed in 2018, this up and coming band fills the room with live energy rock & roll with influences from Bad Company, Beatles, Rolling Stones, Led Zeppelin, Doobie Brothers, Tom Petty, David Bowie, Eddie Money and more.

Coming from a variety of backgrounds, experience and genres, each member of The Risk, brings their own flavor and flair to the sound that together bring a positive vibe and synergy as a whole delivering great energy to any room or venue. Take The Risk and book today for your venue or next event.
